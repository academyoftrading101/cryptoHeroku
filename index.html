Cryptography - Method of using encryption or decryption to secure the communication between the presence of third party without any bad intentions.

Communication requires three main things -

- Computational Algorithm (Hashing algorithm which Bitcoin uses)

- A public key (which a user shares with everyone)

- A private key (digital signature/identity of a user)

Importance of Crypto currency

1) Block Chain- It is a digital exchange of currency. Non reversible and cannot be deleted.

2) Crypto currency Exchange- It means you can exchange crypto currency from normal currency and vice a versa.

3) Miners- Miners are those who ensure security, verify the transaction and checking of accuracy.

4) Wallet- It can be kept on your Drive or External Storage Device.
